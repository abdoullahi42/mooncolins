Burning Mechanism

Each launched token is reduced to 500M supply. 10% of revenue from TMA is allocated to buy back and burn. Tokens collected as fess for Pay App are also burned, except for tokens that have already reached the target 500M supply. 5% of the token supply is burned upon listing on DEX.
