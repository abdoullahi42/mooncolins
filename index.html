How It Works

We are guided by simple step-by-step rules. These rules are our culture.

01

Scheduled Token Launch

We issue tokens with excellent narratives, energetic and strong team on Pump.fun or Moonshot on Saturdays and Sundays at the recommended time by Project Team responsible for handling its narratives to success

02

Initial Buy

To prevent market manipulation and ensure token prosperity, the initial buy for all tokens is set at 20% of the total supply of 1,000,000,000.

03

MPT Allocation

25% of the initial buy (5% of token supply) is allocated to the MPT. It is vested and released gradually to prevent market disruption and ensure a stable token price

04

Founding Team Allocation

25% of the initial buy (5% of token supply) is allocated to founding team members. It is vested and released gradually to prevent market disruption and ensure a stable token price

05

Development and Promotion

50% of the initial buy (10% of token supply) is allocated to fueling the growth and adoption of the token. It is vested and released gradually to prevent market disruption and ensure a stable token price

Token Issuance Framework
